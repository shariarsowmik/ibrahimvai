About Our University

Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology is situated in Qadirabad Cantonment, Natore. Information Communication Engineering is one of the most outstanding department of BAUET. This department started it's journey on 14 March 2016 with only 6 students and 2 lecturers. Now it's a department of more than 150+ students and 10+ lectureres. This department offers great courses about programming, networking and communication.
